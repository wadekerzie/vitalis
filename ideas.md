- Gradient overlays on hero sections (navy to emerald)
- Subtle grid patterns in backgrounds

- Smooth scroll behavior with section anchors
- Hover states that elevate cards with shadows

- Entrance animations for key statistics (counter animations)
- Smooth transitions between sections
- Hover elevations for interactive elements
- Parallax effects on hero background

- Display: Inter Bold (700) for headings - modern, professional
- Body: Inter Regular (400) for paragraphs - highly readable

## Selected Design: Swiss Modernism meets Data Journalism

I'm choosing the first approach because it strikes the perfect balance for a professional business report:
- **Credibility**: Swiss modernism's precision and grid-based layouts convey authority
- **Readability**: Clear hierarchy ensures the dense information is digestible
- **Professional**: Navy and emerald palette feels medical, trustworthy, and growth-oriented
- **Data-Forward**: Design supports the quantitative nature of the report without overwhelming it
