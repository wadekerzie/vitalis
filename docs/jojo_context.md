## 1. Core Markers We Test (Coremarkerswetest.pdf)

### Core Inflammation Markers (drive aging, recovery, metabolic risk)
1. hs-CRP — Primary systemic inflammation marker. Tracks improvement quickly (30–60 days).
2. Ferritin — Dual-purpose: iron storage + inflammatory marker. Helps detect hidden inflammation.
3. Homocysteine — Methylation + cardiovascular risk + inflammatory stress.
Optional upgrade: Fibrinogen (advanced cardiovascular layer).

### Core Metabolic Markers (determine longevity and weight regulation)
4. HbA1c — Long-term glucose control.
5. Fasting Glucose — Immediate metabolic snapshot.
6. Fasting Insulin — Critical for detecting insulin resistance early.
7. TG/HDL Ratio — Powerful cardiometabolic indicator.
8. ApoB (preferred over LDL if available) — Best cardiovascular particle marker.
"These 5–8 markers define metabolic vitality."

### Core Nutrient Markers (justify IV protocols intelligently)
9. Vitamin D (25-OH) — Immune, hormone, inflammation support.
10. B12 — Energy + methylation + neurologic health.
11. Folate — Especially if discussing methylation/HRT.
12. Magnesium (RBC preferred) — Muscle, recovery, stress resilience.
Optional: Omega-3 index (powerful inflammation marker).

### Hormone / HRT Core Panel
For Women (Perimenopause / Menopause):
13. Estradiol (E2)
14. Progesterone (if uterus present)
15. Total Testosterone
16. Free Testosterone
17. SHBG
18. DHEA-S
Add: TSH + Free T3 + Free T4 (thyroid baseline).

For Men (TRT Candidates):
13. Total Testosterone
14. Free Testosterone
15. SHBG
16. Estradiol (sensitive assay)
17. Hematocrit / Hemoglobin
18. PSA (age appropriate)
"These are mandatory safety markers."

### The Foundational 3-Pillar Structure for Vitalis (simplified for branding):
- Inflammation: hs-CRP, Ferritin, Homocysteine
- Metabolic: HbA1c, Insulin, TG/HDL, ApoB
- Nutrient: Vitamin D, B12, Magnesium

"We focus on inflammation, metabolism, nutrient sufficiency, and hormone balance. Everything we do supports one of those four systems."
